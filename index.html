About Us

At Helping Hands NGO, we believe in empowering communities and transforming lives. Since our founding, we have worked hand-in-hand with volunteers and donors to bring hope, education, and healthcare to thousands of underprivileged individuals.

Our mission is simple: “Be the reason someone smiles today.” Through our various outreach programs, we are making small changes that lead to a bigger, brighter future.
